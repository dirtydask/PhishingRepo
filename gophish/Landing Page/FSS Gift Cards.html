Force Support Squadron Gift Cards

FSS News
Tara Correia - Marketing Director
Friday, 12 July 2019
453 Hits
0 Comments

Force Support Squadron (FSS) gift cards make it easy to purchase gifts for family and friends. Best of all, there are no fees to use your card, and no lost value if you don't use it right away! Use your gift card just like cash to make purchases at all participating FSS locations. Your purchases are deducted from the gift card balance and any unused balance will remain on the card for future use.

Where to use your FSS gift card:

  • VAT & UTAP 
  • Auto Hobby Shop/Auto Parts Yard
  • Club Eifel: Catering, Saber Sports Lounge, Events (Cash Cage)
  • FSS Food & Beverage: Burrito Bandito, Eifel Grind, Club House Grill, Hangar 52, Pizza Hut Express, Kühl Beanz/Kühl Beanz 2, Papa Joe's
  • Airman & Family Readiness Center 
  • Eifel Community Center: Music Lessons, Brick House Rental, Passport Photos
  • Spangdahlem Library 
  • Saber Pet Lodge 
  • Arts & Crafts: Crafts & Party Central and Plaques Plus
  • Eifel Lanes Bowling Center 
  • Eifel Mountain Golf Course/Pro-Shop
  • Spangdahlem Fitness Center 
  • Outdoor Recreation
  • Eifel Arms Inn Lodging
  • Information, Tickets & Travel
  • Youth Programs: School Age Program, Pre-Teen & Teen Center, Youth Sports
  • Child Development Center 
 
 
How to win an FSS gift card: 
 
 
  • FSS Weekly Newsletter: Every Friday the 52d FSS Marketing Department sends out a weekly email to our subscribers. The email is filled with information on upcoming events, programs, services, new hours of operation and other helpful information. Our subscribers are automatically entered to win when they sign up! The winner, also known as the Fan of the Week, is announced at the bottom of each newsletter. 
  • Find Felix in the FLASH: We hide our mascot, Felix, somewhere inside every issue of the FLASH magazine. If you find him, simply send us an email with the page number and location of Felix to . Your name will be entered into the drawing and we will send the winner an email at the end of the month. 
  • FSS Events and Contest: Check out our monthly calendar available under the "home" tab or by clicking the calendar icon located at the top right of the home page. We have a lot of fun events coming up with plenty of chances to win an FSS gift card. 
 

Gift Card Limitations:

Gift cards cannot be redeemed for online purchases or for cash. The cards cannot be used in Army, Air Force Exchange, the Commissary, Lodging, and some FSS facilities. Unused Gift Card balances cannot be transferred.

The United States Air Force and its affiliates make no warranties express or implied, with respect to gift cards, including without limitation, any express or implied warranty of merchantability or fitness for a particular purpose. In the event a gift card code does not function, your sole remedy and our sole liability, shall be the replacement of such gift card.

No Air Force entity or affiliate is responsible if a Gift Card is lost, stolen, destroyed or used without your permission.​
