Cruise Specials

ITT is working together with the Italian Cruise Company MSC.
Due to quick changes in availabilities and prices, we ask you to come in person to our office for getting a quote or a reservation.
Please be prepared in which route/region you are interested in, have the preferred dates and number of adults/ children with you.
For booking, we need the tourist passports of all travelers.

SPECIAL BREAK CRUISE OFFER with MSC LIRICA

from 08 to 13 May 2019 traveling the Eastern Mediterranean Sea from Venice to Zadar - Kotor - Koper - Ravenna. Price is starting from 469 Euros per person in an inside cabin staying with 2 persons. Price includes cabin stay and meals. Single supplement, transfer to the harbor and back and drink packages are available against a surcharge.
